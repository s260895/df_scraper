Detailed description of the Changa:

Changa, the smokable ayahuasca:
The DMT herbal mixture is easier and more pleasant to smoke than pure freebase DMT. A simple pipe or bong can be used and the taste and aroma are much better. In addition, the ingredients each bring their own character to the trip, enrich the experience and make for a pleasant afterglow.

The mixture consists of 50% N,N-DMT (freebase), 20% Calea Zacatechichi (Aztec Dream Herb), 20% Damiana (Turnera Diffusa) and 10% Peganum Harmala Extract (freebase, MAO-Inhibitor)

We extract and base our highly potent N,N-DMT from the root bark of the mimosa hostilis in our laboratory. Over time, we have found that there are noticeable and qualitative differences depending on the plant from which we extract the DMT and the region from which the plant originates. Therefore, we have now decided to use Mimosa Hostilis which we obtain directly from the farmers of a Mayan village in western Guatemala. We make sure that there is no indigenous abuse and that the cultivation is ecological.

We add 10% of a Peganum Harmala extract which we have basified for the optimal experience. The alkaloids from this serve as an MAO inhibitor and extend the duration of the trip up to 20 minutes and give it more depth. The break-through is also easier to achieve and can be smoked more often in a row. In contrast to pure DMT, the intoxication is decelerated and has a certain ayahuasca characteristic.

The Aztec Dream Herb rounds off the break-through with its own plant spirit and helps to reflect it again in the following. We have found that it allows you to access what you have experienced just before falling asleep and to have vivid, lucid dreams during the night. The well-known effect of the fast "forgetting" that often occurs after a pure DMT trip is thus diminished. Especially in the morning hours after waking up, you can also feel the effect again. Don't worry, the effect of the Dream Herb is by no means another unexpected trip, but only creates a gentle feeling and awareness of the experience.

Damiana (Turnera Diffusa) is known for its stimulating and mood-lifting effects and is traditionally used against depressive moods and anxiety disorders. However, it also brings a very pleasant aroma and rounds off the smoke of the Changa tastefully.

recommended use:
Provided the set & setting is correct, sit down and take a few deep breaths, empty your lungs completely. If you have an open pipe that brings the Changa in direct contact with the flame, use a lighter with a low flame and touch the Changa lightly with it while inhaling slowly and continuously. If you smoke in an oil pipe with which the Changa does not come in direct contact with the fire, you need a storm lighter (jetflame). Fill your lungs completely, draw some more air and hold it for 5-10 seconds. Exhale relaxed, take a few deep breaths and repeat the whole thing twice more. After the second round you will already feel the effect of the first inhalation, if you feel able to do so and want to travel deeper, try to inhale a third time. This way you can also achieve a complete and very interesting break-through experience. It is helpful to have another person assisting you, holding your pipe and fire, so that you can let yourself fall directly afterwards, lie down and close your eyes and in case of doubt motivate yourself to take a third or fourth move.
